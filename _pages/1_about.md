I am a computer science PhD-track student at [Institut Polytechnique de Paris](https://www.ip-paris.fr/). I am currently working on my M1 internship in the [QURIOSITY team](https://quriosity.telecom-paris.fr/), under the supervision of [Peter Brown](https://peterjbrown519.github.io/).

Generally, I am interested in working in the intersection of theoretical computer science, mathematics, and physics, and using the insight gained from one to contribute to the advancement of another.

Specifically, I am interested in:

*   Quantum Computational Complexity
*   Quantum Information Theory
*   Quantum Cryptography

I am also an amateur digital artist, writer and poet.

I prefer They/Them pronouns, but He/Him also works.
